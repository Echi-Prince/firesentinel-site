About FireSentinel

FireSentinel is an AlexNet‑powered system for early fire and smoke detection. It runs on edge devices or the cloud and can integrate with existing CCTV systems. When detection confidence crosses a threshold tuned for high recall, alerts are dispatched immediately to authorities and site operators.

Model & Training

  • Transfer learning from ImageNet with fine‑tuning
  • Strong augmentations; focal loss for imbalance
  • Temporal smoothing for video stability
  • Grad‑CAM explainability for audits

Deployment

Export as TorchScript/ONNX; supports INT8 quantization. Integrates via RTSP/ONVIF/HTTP and REST APIs.
